Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Our FAQ section provides quick answers to common queries about insurance policies, claims, renewals, and more. Find the information you need to make informed decisions about your coverage.

What types of insurance plans do you offer?

We offer term insurance, investment plans, life insurance, ULIP plans, and NRI-specific policies to suit different needs.

How can I file a claim?

You can file a claim online through our website, via our customer service helpline, or by visiting the nearest branch with the required documents.

How do I check my policy status?

You can check your policy status by logging into your account on our website or contacting our customer service team.

What documents are required to buy an insurance policy?

Generally, you will need identity proof, address proof, income proof (for some plans), and recent photographs.

Can I change my nominee after purchasing a policy?

Yes, you can update your nominee details anytime by submitting a request online or visiting our branch with the required documents.

How can I renew my policy?

You can renew your policy online through our website, via net banking, UPI, or by visiting our branch before the due date.
